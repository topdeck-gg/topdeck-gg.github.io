Classes

Deck Control

These decks are focused on holding the matches until their winning conditions are acquired, in general, that winning conditions are combinations of cards that lead to victory if used together, for example, The Dreadway + Commander Ledros, which is one of the combinations that if placed on the field, will lead to an almost certain victory.

Deck Aggro

These decks are made to be aggressive in their strategies, Aggro aims to win the game even before the opponent develops his own strategy, only aims at damage to the enemy nexus, one of its examples is Burn / Teemo Aggro which has numerous low-cost followers who have the ability to easily damage the enemy's "face" in addition to many spells that can complete the damage to achieve quick and easy victory.

Deck Midrange

This type of deck has the flexibility to behave almost like an Aggro, almost like a Control, because this is like an average of both, neither so aggressive nor so defensive, it tends to win games faster than a Control but not so much as for an Aggro, as an example we have the Midrange Shen that applies constant pressure with incessant attack, however it has shields that protect its own followers and champions, which makes it a fun and versatile deck to play.
